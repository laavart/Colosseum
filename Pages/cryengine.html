CryENGINE

What is CryENGINE?

CryEngine (officially stylized as CRYENGINE)
is a game engine designed by the German game
developer Crytek. It has been used in all of
their titles with the initial version being
used in Far Cry, and continues to be updated
to support new consoles and hardware for
their games.
