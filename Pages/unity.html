Unity Engine

What is Unity Engine?

Unity is a cross-platform game engine developed
by Unity Technologies, first announced and released
in June 2005 at Apple Inc.'s Worldwide Developers
Conference as a Mac OS X-exclusive game engine.
As of 2018, the engine had been extended to support
more than 25> platforms.
